Our Story

A Homely Stay in
the Heart of Umbergaon

Hotel Aaram was born from a simple belief — that every traveller deserves a space that feels warm, clean, and genuinely welcoming. Nestled on Station Road in Umbergaon's GIDC area, we've been the trusted choice for business travellers, families, and visitors exploring Gujarat's coastal belt.

"Aaram" means rest — and that is exactly what we promise. From our homely restaurant to our comfortable, air-conditioned rooms, everything here is designed around your peace of mind.
